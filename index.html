Welcome

I am Biswanath, a PhD Candidate at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. My primary research interest focuses on the representation of lexical categories. Currently, I am exploring the flexibility of categorization in Munda languages in India.

I work under the supervision of Prof. Pritha Chandra as a part of the SynDaC research group.
