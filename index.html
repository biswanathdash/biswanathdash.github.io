Welcome

I am an Assistant Professor in the Dept of Humanities and Social Sciences (HSS) at IIT Delhi. I am also associated with the Cognitive Science MSc program.

Along with my students, my research areas are computational lexical semantics, argument structure and lexical access. We are also interested in the design, collection and evaluation of (linguistic) datasets for NLP.

Our focus is on the languages of the Indian subcontinent. We have worked on Hindi, Marathi, Urdu and Malayalam.

We work in these areas using both computational and experimental methods.
